Title: Social Media Do’s and Don’ts
✅ Do’s

Think before posting anything online
Maintain a professional profile and image
Use privacy settings wisely
Verify information before sharing
Be respectful and positive in interactions
❌ Don’ts

Don’t share personal or sensitive information
Don’t engage in online arguments or hate speech
Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content
Don’t spread fake news or rumors
Don’t overshare your location or daily routine
