✅ Stay Safe Online – Checklist for Students
✔️ Do not click on suspicious links in emails, SMS, or WhatsApp messages

✔️ Always check the sender’s email address before trusting any message

✔️ Use strong, unique passwords for different accounts

✔️ Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible

✔️ Never share OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone

💰 UPI / Financial Safety Tips
✔️ Never approve unknown UPI collect requests

✔️ Do not scan random QR codes to receive money (you only scan to pay)

✔️ Verify payment requests before sending money

✔️ Use official banking apps only (avoid third-party unknown apps)

🛡️ General Safety
✔️ Keep your devices updated with latest security patches

✔️ Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or sensitive transactions

✔️ Log out from shared/public computers

✔️ Be cautious of “too good to be true” offers
